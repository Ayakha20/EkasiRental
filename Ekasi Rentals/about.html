About Ekasi Rentals

History

Ekasi Rentals was created in 2026 after identifying the need for reliable platform where people in township can easily find rental accomodation. Many Rental opportunities are shared through word of mouth or social media, which can confuse or scam. Ekasi Rentals was developed to make it easier, transparent, and safer for landlords and tenants to connect online.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe and simple online rental platform that helps residents find affordable rental accomodation.

Vision

Our vision is to become the most trusted online rental platform for township communities across South Africa

Target Audience

  • Students seeking rooms or shared housing
  • Young proffessionals lookfing for backrooms or small housing options
  • Families looking for affordable homes
  • Landlords who want to advertise their rental properties online
